## 💡 ИДЕЯ СТАНДАРТА

**Формат:** `DK [Category] | [Details]`

**Принципы:**
- **Английский** — для **отображаемого имени** канала в Telegram; **11 логических каналов** (не 22/33)
- RU/KA — в описании, закрепе и сообщениях, **не** отдельными каналами с тем же смыслом
- Единый формат для всех каналов
- Префикс `DK` для брендинга
- Разделитель `|` для категоризации
- **Один канал на тему** — все сотрудники в одной группе
- Внутри каналов общение на всех языках (RU/EN/KA)

## 📝 ОПИСАНИЯ КАНАЛОВ (EN)

### DK Warehouse | Orders
**Description:** Internal channel for warehouse operations management. Orders, invoices, product arrivals. Information about availability and deliveries.  
**Audience:** Warehouse staff, procurement managers, office managers  
**Content:** Invoices, orders, delivery information, product availability

### DK Sales
**Description:** General sales channel. Information about new arrivals, available products, promotions for employees. Main communication channel between office and sales points.  
**Audience:** All employees involved in sales, sales point managers, office managers  
**Content:** New arrivals, available products, information from office, promotions

### DK Inventory | [Location]
**Description:** Channel for accounting and inventory at specific sales point. Shift handover, sales reports, open customer cards, bonus control. Used when accounting system is down.  
**Audience:** Sales point staff, point managers  
**Content:** Sales reports, shift handover information, open customer cards, inventory, bonus control  
**Locations:** Paliashvili, Batumi, Gulvil, East Point

### DK Regulations
**Description:** Channel with regulations, rules and work standards of DimKava. Internal documents, procedures, customer service standards. All current company regulations.  
**Audience:** All company employees, new employees, managers  
**Content:** Work regulations, service standards, rules and procedures, regulation updates, internal documents

### DK Training
**Description:** Channel for training and development of employees. Materials for trainees, training materials, information about trainings and master classes. Everything needed for professional growth.  
**Audience:** Trainees, new employees, all employees for professional development  
**Content:** Training materials, instructions for trainees, training information, master classes, skill development materials

### DK Content
**Description:** Channel for collecting content for social media and marketing. Photos, videos, post ideas. Each employee must submit content according to regulations (e.g., 3 photos/videos from each employee).  
**Audience:** All employees, marketing department, content managers  
**Content:** Product photos, process videos, post ideas, social media content, user-generated content

### DK Cleaning | Equipment
**Description:** Channel for monitoring coffee machine cleaning. Employees must send photos/reports of equipment cleaning according to schedule (Tuesday and Friday). Quality control of equipment maintenance.  
**Audience:** Baristas, employees working with equipment, sales point managers  
**Content:** Equipment cleaning photos, cleaning reports, cleaning schedule information, equipment issues  
**Frequency:** Tuesday and Friday

### DK Latte Art
**Description:** Channel for demonstrating latte art skills. Employees send latte art photos from each sales point for quality control and skill development. Minimum 1 photo from each point daily.  
**Audience:** Baristas, all employees working with coffee, managers  
**Content:** Latte art photos, latte art creation videos, latte art ideas  
**Frequency:** Daily (minimum 1 photo from each point)
